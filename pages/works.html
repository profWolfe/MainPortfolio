A step by step process in creating, maintaining, and enjoying a live ecosystem.

Some things in mind when starting out with an aquarium:

The aquarium

When choosing the size of the aquarium the larger the size the better. When housing fish in an aquarium the larger the aquarium is the easier it can be to maintain long term, keep fish happy and healthy, and has a great look when compaired to the smaller counterparts.

The filter

There are many types of filters and ways to filer your water to keep the most prestine water permaters. A few notable filters are the overhead, canister, and sump filters. Each serving uses based on size(dimensions) of the tank to types of fish.

The substrate

Substrate can provide a quality bed for your fish. Depending on the type of fish you are keeping choosing your substrate is key. for example, when choosing a fish like a corydora, sand or round peballs can leave your corydora happy because they are a bottom dwelling fish and are always sifting through the substrate.
